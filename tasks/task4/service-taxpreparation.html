Tax Preparation

Preparing your own tax return can be extremely difficult with today’s complex tax laws and the constant changes to regulations and legislation. According to a study released by the US Government’s General Accounting Office last year, most taxpayers (77% of 71 million taxpayers) believe they benefited from using a professional tax preparer.

For many, filing a relatively simple return can be confusing, even if relying on computer software tools to help. By doing your taxes yourself, you take the risk of overlooking deductions and credits that you are entitled to. For the peace-of-mind and deep expertise, turn to our professionals to prepare your tax return.

At Jeanine Hemingway, CPA we have the expertise that is needed to help you file your returns while also helping you to minimize your tax liability with careful planning.

Our Tax Services For Businesses Include:

  • Business Tax Return Preparation and Planning
  • Corporate and Partnership Tax Planning and Return Preparation
  • Audit Preparation
  • Filing of Tax Forms

Our Tax Services For Individuals:

  • Federal Return
  • Multi-state Return
  • Prior Year(s) Return
