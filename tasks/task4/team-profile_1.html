Jeanine Hemingway

Founder

Jeanine received a degree in Accounting from Indiana University and has been a Certified Public Accountant since 1996. In 2001, she started her own accounting firm, specializing in accounting, bookkeeping, training and tax services to individuals and small businesses. Jeanine is Treasurer of the Bowie Orchestra Boosters, along with being an active member of South Austin Barracudas Swim Team and South Austin Business Association (SABA).
